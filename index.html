About

I'm a software engineer, and I knew it ever since my parents got me
a Super Nintendo back in the day. I was totally smitten by what could
be done in a small little box. Fast-forward to today, I am experienced in
JavaScript, Ruby on Rails, React, and Redux.

Learning how things work is simply in my veins, and so I'm always
striving to understand how applications work from beginning to end.
I love to tinker and see how one small change can help craft intuitive experiences.

Whenever I'm not working on my next masterpiece, you can find me
checking out the latest trends and designs in the gaming industry.
